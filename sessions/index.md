# Parallel session 

After lunch there will be parallel sessions at Akureyri Junior College. 

* The *Contributed session* is for talks that do not fit into one of the other sessions. If you wish to give a contributed talk, please send a title and abstract to the Congress Chair at [isf@stae.is](mailto:isf@stae.is) by DEADLINE.
* Below is a list of confirmed sessions. Active Nordic research communities are invited to propose additional sessions.  If you wish to organize an additional session, please submit a proposal to the Congress Chair at [isf@stae.is](mailto:isf@stae.is) by 30 September 2026, including:
   - Title of the proposed session.
   - Names, affiliations, and e-mail addresses of the organizers (at least 2).
   - Description of current research activity in the area and the Nordic and international community that would underpin the session.
   - List of possible speakers (at least 10).
* The Scientific Committee will assess proposals for additional sessions and respond to proposers by 31 October 2026.  The Committee will expect gender diversity of speakers and representation from at least two Nordic countries, and will seek to minimise overlap with other sessions.
